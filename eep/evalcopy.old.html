Getting an Evaluation Copy of the Book

Qualified instructors can easily get an evaluation copy of the text (ISBN: 0070059527) and the Instructor's Manual (ISBN: 0070059535) from McGraw Hill in any of the following ways:

  • Contact your local McGraw Hill College representative.
  • Phone McGraw Hill at (800) 338-3987, and follow the path for "getting an examination copy."
  • McGraw Hill is currently modifying its web site, so this method may not work at the moment. Request a copy electronically by filling out the following form.
