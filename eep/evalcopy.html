Getting an Evaluation Copy of the Book

Qualified instructors can easily get an evaluation copy of the text (ISBN: 0070059527) and the Instructor's Manual (ISBN: 0070059535) from Irwin/McGraw-Hill in any of the following ways:

  • Contact your local Irwin/McGraw-Hill College representative.
  • Phone Irwin/McGraw-Hill at (800) 338-3987, and follow the path for "getting an examination copy."
  • Request a copy via email to listens@irwin.com.
  • Request a copy electronically via Irwin/McGraw-Hill's website, or try this more direct form.
  • You can buy a single copy.
