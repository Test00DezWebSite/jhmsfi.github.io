We are going to test the new online experiment system called EconX. I realize that this is a bit of a bother, but I do want to get your feedback on the new system given your experience with EconU.

  • Go to the EconX web site.

  • When it asks for the "Course Admit Code" enter "110" (but without the quotes, that is, just the number 110).
  • You should be all set from there, and things will look more or less familar.
  • When you go back to the site, you no longer need to register, just click on the "LOG IN" button.
