Advising

Please note, the advising system has changed for IS majors. All advising for IS majors is now being handled by William Hefley, PH 208G.
All new advisees should make sure they read and fully understand my advising statement . If, after reading this statement, you feel that you cannot abide by it, then come and see me immediately.

All advisees should make sure that they have submitted an advisee form . The first time you submit the form, please fill it out completely . At the beginning of each subsequent academic year, you should submit a new form by completing the ``name'' section and updating only those sections that have changed since the previous form.
